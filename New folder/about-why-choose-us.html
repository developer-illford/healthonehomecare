Why Choose Health One Group?

A Trusted Partner for Healthcare and Support Services

We understand how important it is for care providers to have dependable, skilled staff who can deliver high-quality care. Our agency is committed to providing staffing solutions that organisations can rely on.

Carefully Selected Professionals

All our staff go through a thorough recruitment and vetting process, ensuring they meet professional standards and are fully prepared to work in healthcare and support environments.

Skilled and Trained Staff

We ensure our staff receive relevant training and ongoing support so they can confidently meet the needs of residents and service users.

Reliable and Flexible Staffing

Whether you require last-minute shift cover, temporary staff, or permanent recruitment, we provide flexible solutions tailored to your organisation.

Person-Centred Approach

Our staff are trained to deliver care that respects dignity, independence, and individual needs, ensuring every resident and service user receives compassionate support.

Continuity and Consistency

Where possible, we provide regular staff members to the same services, helping build trust and familiarity with residents and service users.

Supporting Organisations Across Northern Ireland

We proudly work with care homes, hostels, and assisted living services, helping them maintain safe, supportive environments for the people in their care.
