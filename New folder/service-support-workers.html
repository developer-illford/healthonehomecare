Support Workers for Hostels & Supported Living

Compassionate and independence-focused care for vulnerable individuals

We supply experienced support workers to hostels, supported accommodation, and assisted living services across Northern Ireland.

Our staff help individuals manage daily life, build confidence, and maintain safety while promoting dignity, stability, and long-term independence.

Areas we support

Mental health challenges Learning disabilities Autism spectrum conditions Substance misuse recovery Homelessness and housing support Physical disabilities and behavioural support needs

Our workers are trained in safeguarding, person-centred care, communication, and healthy professional boundaries. They offer practical help with daily living skills, appointments, routines, medication prompts, meal preparation, and emotional reassurance.
