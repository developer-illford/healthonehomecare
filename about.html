Susan’s Story

37- Senior Healthcare Assistant, Health One.

I’m Susan, a single mother with a little boy and twin girls. For years, I had taken a break from my career as a senior support worker to raise my children, but I always dreamed of returning to my job.

When I heard about Health One Group Healthcare Agency, I was excited to apply for a position. I couldn’t believe my luck when I was offered a role as a senior support worker. I was over the moon that I could finally return to my dream job.

Working for Health One Group Healthcare Agency has been a game-changer for me. The agency provided me with flexible working hours that suited my schedule as a single mother with young children. I could choose shifts that allowed me to drop off and pick up my children from and attend their important events. I no longer had to worry about missing out on special moments with my children.

Health One Group Healthcare Agency is more than just an agency; it’s a supportive community of like-minded professionals who care about me and my family. I have found that the agency has a culture of support, where everyone is there to help each other out. I felt like I was part of a team that truly cared about me and my family.

Returning to my dream job as a senior support worker with Health One Group Healthcare Agency has been a life-changing experience. I can now achieve a work-life balance that allows me to be a present mother while still pursuing my career goals. I would highly recommend Health One Group Healthcare Agency to any healthcare professional looking for a supportive and flexible work environment.

FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. Who Do You Supply Healthcare Staff To?

    We’re a multifaceted healthcare agency and we have experience in supplying passionate, high-quality healthcare professionals for a range of roles in Northern Ireland. We supply our teams to the private sector and also routinely work with the NHS to supply great healthcare support to the general public.

  • 2. I Have Roles I Need To Fill, How Quickly Can You Do This?

    We understand that it’s important for you to get talented staff into your roles quickly as understaffing can pose serious risks and difficulties for healthcare providers. Once you’ve highlighted your vacancies to us, we will aim to have your shifts covered by our team of Health One experts within 15-minutes.

  • 3. What Kind of Roles Do You Have Available?

    We have a range of temporary and permanent roles available to make sure you can always find the right job for you. You can see some examples of your next great role down below. Nursing Healthcare Assistants Social Workers Occupational Therapists Physiotherapists Specialist Healthcare Chefs Domestic Roles Paramedics Speech and Language Therapists

  • 4. How Long Does Your Recruitment Process Usually Take?

    It can be difficult to pin down exactly how long your recruitment process may take, we endeavour to process your application and get you into work as soon as possible. The most lengthy part of the process is the return of Access NI Enhanced Disclosure Certificate, which can take up to two weeks. After this, you’ll be in your new role before you know it!

  • 5. Will I Need To Wear a Uniform When Working?

    At Health One Group, we take pride in our work and professionalism seriously. Because of this, you will be required to wear a uniform when working with us. Not to worry though, all of our uniforms are provided for free!

  • 6. I Have Flexible Working Needs, Is This Possible?

    We understand the importance of balancing life and work. We always try to make adjustments to help our team in their work, if you have flexible working needs we have shifts that can fit around your schedule.

  • 7. Who Are You Regulated By?

    We fall under regulation in Northern Ireland, being assessed by the Regulation and Quality Improvement Authority (RQIA).
