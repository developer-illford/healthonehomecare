Join Health One Group Agency and Become a Hero of Healthcare

Welcome to Health One, where we are dedicated to providing the highest quality healthcare professionals to the NHS, care homes and charitable organisations across Northern Ireland. We understand the importance of finding the right balance between work and life, which is why we prioritise work-life balance for all our staff.

Our agency is proud to offer a diverse range of opportunities for nurses, healthcare assistants, occupational therapists, social workers, paramedics and other healthcare professionals. We’re made up of the best and brightest in our field and we’re committed to providing ongoing training and support so you’re always equipped with the latest knowledge and skills.

At Health One Healthcare Agency we believe that our staff are our greatest asset. This is why we’ll look after you with great rates of pay, flexible working opportunities and a supportive work environment to help you grow and reach your full potential. Whether you’re a seasoned healthcare professional or looking for a change of pace, or even a new graduate starting your career- we’ll have opportunities that are perfect for you.

So, if you’re looking for a healthcare agency that truly values your skills, experience and personal wellbeing- look no further than Health One. Join our team today and take the first step towards a rewarding career in healthcare where you really are number ONE.

The Traits of a Health One Professional

Caring and passionate about providing quality service for our clients. Dedication to self-improvement and further training. A team player that lifts their colleagues and gets the best out of them. The ability to question the status quo and help drive innovation and improvements. Having the ability to maintain professionalism at all times whilst being personable.
