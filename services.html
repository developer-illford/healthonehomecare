Care Home Staffing

Ancillary Staff Support

In addition to care and support staff, we provide:

Chefs and Kitchen Assistants for nutritious meal preparation Cleaning staff to maintain hygiene and safety standards

This ensures that care homes and supported living facilities operate smoothly and maintain the highest standards of care, safety, and comfort.

Shift Staffing

We know it can be stressful and difficult to find talented healthcare staff at the drop of the hat. That’s why we’ve modelled the way we work to make sure you get qualified and skilled workers as soon as you need them, 24/7, 365-days a year. The entire process is easy from start-to-finish and once we’ve onboarded you as a client, you’ll be able to get your shifts filled within 15-minutes of making a request.

Send your requirements to our team on email or over the phone. Get suggestions for staff based nearby to you based on your needs. Once we have the best candidate, we’ll give you their full professional profile. As shifts are completed, they’ll be automatically updated in your profile. Your ongoing shifts will continue to be effectively managed by us.

Activities & Resident Engagement

We believe meaningful engagement is as important as physical care. Our staff provide personalised activities tailored to each resident’s interests and abilities, including:

Reminiscence sessions Reading and conversation Arts and crafts Music and singing Gentle exercises Games and cognitive stimulation One-to-one social interaction

This approach helps reduce isolation and anxiety while promoting dignity, confidence, and joy.

Short-Notice & Emergency Cover

We respond quickly and professionally to urgent staffing needs, offering:

Same-day emergency cover Short-term placements Ongoing rota support Day, night, and weekend shifts

Our fast and reliable service ensures safe staffing levels and continuity of care.

Support Workers for Hostels & Supported Living

We supply experienced Support Workers to hostels and supported living facilities, providing care that is compassionate, resilient, and independence focused.

The Role of Our Support Workers

Our staff support individuals experiencing:

Homelessness Mental health challenges Learning disabilities Substance recovery needs Social vulnerability

Their responsibilities include:

Providing practical daily living support Encouraging structure and positive routines Offering emotional reassurance and motivation Supporting attendance at appointments Promoting independence and life skills Maintaining professional boundaries and safeguarding Our aim is to empower individuals to build confidence, stability, and long-term independence.

1:1 Specialist Care Support

We supply experienced healthcare assistants and registered nurses to private care homes across Northern Ireland, with a strong focus on high-quality one-to-one care

We provide dedicated one-to-one support for residents requiring enhanced supervision, reassurance, or complex care. Our services include:

Continuous supervision to reduce falls risk Dementia support and reassurance Emotional support for anxious or distressed residents Waking night observation End-of-life companionship and care

Our personalised approach enhances safety, emotional wellbeing, and overall quality of life.

Permanent Staffing

If you’re looking for help with managing your hiring process for permanent roles, we can also help with this easily, the process is short and sweet..

Give us all of the details about the role. We’ll help you to write the job advert to entice candidates. Let us do the heavy lifting of advertising your role for you in our marketing channels. We’ll then shortlist the best candidates and arrange interviews. Now, you have the perfect person to fill your vacancy.

If you’re ready to find your next team of healthcare professionals to support your organisation, arrange an appointment with us and you’ll be in the safest possible hands.
